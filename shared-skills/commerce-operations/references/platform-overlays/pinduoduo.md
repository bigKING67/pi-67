# Pinduoduo Operations Overlay

## Use when

Use for channel-exclusive packs, value-channel execution, clearance, price governance, logistics, service, refunds, or unauthorized-distribution monitoring.

## Operating system

- Enforce SKU, packaging, specification, barcode, price, and channel-role separation.
- Monitor subsidy source, freight, service, complaints, reviews, returns, and cross-channel price comparison.
- Use controlled inventory and settlement rules for clearance or special channels.
- Track unauthorized sellers and route evidence to channel governance owners.

## Exceptions

Stop when the same hero SKU breaks price rules, logistics destroys contribution, complaints rise, or unauthorized distribution cannot be controlled.
