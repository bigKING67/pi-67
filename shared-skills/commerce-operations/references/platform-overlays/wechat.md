# WeChat and Private-Domain Operations Overlay

## Use when

Use for video-account live operations, mini-program, membership, private-domain tagging, service, community cadence, repeat purchase, or complaints.

## Operating system

- Define identity, consent, tags, lifecycle state, service owner, content cadence, commerce destination, and escalation.
- Coordinate video/live, mini-program, community, customer service, membership, replenishment, and recall.
- Maintain contact policy, opt-out, privacy, complaint, and data-access controls.

## Exceptions

Pause automation or campaigns when consent, privacy, unsubscribe, complaints, service load, stock, or fulfillment fails. Current capabilities require verification.
