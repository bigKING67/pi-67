# Douyin Operations Overlay

## Use when

Use for Douyin self-live, talent-live execution, product cards, store/shelf operations, campaign readiness, or after-sale operations.

## Operating system

- Self-live: opening, hero, bundle/profit, benefit, and repeat sequence with room-level checkpoints.
- Talent live: product fit, inventory, price, benefit, script, samples, authorization, settlement, refund, and review plan.
- Shelf/store: title, cover, SKU, price, reviews, service, fulfillment, and search landing consistency.
- Campaign: product setup, stock, gifts, host/service staffing, escalation, and recovery.

## Core metrics

Track qualified visitors, retention, product clicks, payment CVR, AOV, GPM/UV value where available, refunds, service load, inventory, natural share, and contribution implications.

## Exceptions

Pause or downgrade when price rules, inventory, claims, refund, fulfillment, or service capacity fail. Current platform workflow and fields require verification.
