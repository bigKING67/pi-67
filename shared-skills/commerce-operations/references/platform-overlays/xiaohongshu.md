# Xiaohongshu Operations Overlay

## Use when

Use for creator delivery operations, note approval, comments, brand account cadence, search landing coordination, or commerce handoff.

## Operating system

- Maintain creator roster, briefs, samples, deadlines, review status, rights, publication, amplification, and expiry metadata.
- Review claims and evidence without making every creator asset identical.
- Track comments and search signals, then update keyword, FAQ, content, and landing actions.
- Coordinate off-platform or in-platform landing, price consistency, and customer-service readiness.

## Exceptions

Escalate false experience, unsupported claims, disclosure issues, rights gaps, abnormal comments, or price/channel conflict. Current workflow and platform controls require verification.
