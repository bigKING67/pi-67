# Kuaishou Operations Overlay

## Use when

Use for creator/live execution, relationship-commerce cadence, creator-specific packs, shelf/search operations, fulfillment, or after-sale.

## Operating system

- Coordinate creator relationship, scripts, samples, product packs, price, inventory, service, and repeated-session review.
- Preserve practical explanation and audience trust instead of copying another platform's live rhythm.
- Connect short video, live, shelf, search, and repeat purchase operations.

## Exceptions

Stop or renegotiate when price conflict, commission, refunds, inventory, fulfillment, or after-sale pressure exceed the plan. Current platform workflow requires verification.
