# Endorsement Plan Contract

Use for spokesperson, ambassador, friend-of-brand, celebrity, athlete, expert, cultural figure, or campaign partner decisions.

## Decision fields

- Partnership objective and role type.
- Candidate and alternatives.
- Brand, audience, category, culture, and channel fit.
- Awareness, affinity, credibility, distinctiveness, and overexposure.
- Existing endorsements and competitive conflicts.
- Reputation, conduct, legal, platform, and scenario risks.
- Rights: name, likeness, voice, content, edits, derivatives, paid media, commerce, offline, territory, channels, and term.
- Exclusivity, renewal, takedown, suspension, crisis, and exit provisions to review.
- Fee, activation, production, media, legal, retail, and opportunity cost.
- Announcement, campaign, content, commerce, event, PR, and membership activation.
- Brand, earned, search, content, commerce, and asset-reuse measurement.
- Decision, conditions, vetoes, owner, approval gate, and review date.

Do not recommend a candidate from popularity alone. Final contracts require qualified legal review.
