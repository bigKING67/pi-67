Decision: 不建议直接翻倍；只能小比例测试。

Confirmed facts: GMV 300000, ad spend 150000, ROI 2.0, gross margin 55%, gifts and fulfillment 12% of GMV, refund loss 6%.

Economics: available margin before ad = 55% - 12% - 6% = 37%. Break-even ROI = 1 / 0.37 = 2.70. Gross-profit ROI after gifts/fulfillment/refund is about 0.74, so ROI 2.0 is below break-even ROI.

Assumptions: platform fee, service fee, and content cost are not included; if they exist, break-even ROI is higher.

Risk: marginal traffic may be worse than last month, refund may rise, and price-channel conflict can damage Tmall/Douyin profit.

Stop rule: pause scale if marginal ROI is below 2.70, refund loss rises above 6%, or payment CVR falls. Scale rule: add budget only in 10%-20% increments after marginal ROI exceeds break-even and refund stays stable.
