# Douyin Playbook

## Load when

Use this reference for Douyin self-live, talent livestream, short video, product card, search, mall, Qianchuan, Xingtu, creator selection, or Douyin ROI diagnosis.

## Self-live role

Self-live is the brand-owned conversion hub:

- Convert short-video and Qianchuan traffic.
- Catch users after creator seeding and search return.
- Test assortment, price, script, benefit, and live-room conversion.
- Build fans, stable daily sales, and repurchase.

Track room visitors, entry cost, average watch time, interaction, follow rate, product click rate, payment CVR, GPM, UV value, AOV, refund rate, new-customer share, fan transaction share, Qianchuan ROI, and natural traffic share.

## Self-live product rhythm

Use a compact product sequence:

1. Opening traffic SKU: retention and first click.
2. Main hero SKU: pain point, proof, benefit, urgency.
3. Bundle/profit SKU: raise AOV.
4. Benefit SKU: create peak transaction.
5. Loop hero + profit + fan SKU.

Diagnose:

- Low entry: traffic/creative/cover weakness.
- Low retention: opening, host, or scene weakness.
- Low product click: SKU, cover image, price, benefit, or sequence weakness.
- High click but low order: price, review, trust, gift, or page issue.
- Low ROI: audience mismatch or weak conversion.
- ROI drops after scale: marginal traffic worsens; split plans and improve conversion.

## Talent livestream

Treat talent livestream as a sales channel, not a loose "creator cooperation".

Fit is strong when the product has simple selling points, visual demo, fast decision, strong benefit, enough commission space, stable inventory, controlled refund risk, clear explanation, and acceptable review base.

Avoid heavy bets when the product has high AOV, complex efficacy, low margin, weak price power, unstable supply, few reviews, high after-sale risk, high compliance risk, or unclear repurchase path.

Creator tiers:

- Head: burst and brand endorsement.
- Mid-tier: stable volume and ROI testing.
- Vertical: precise audience and trust.
- Value creator: benefit-driven conversion and clearance.
- KOC: low-cost long-tail proof.
- Group leader/agency: scaled distribution and management.
- Short-live linked creator: short-video warmup plus live conversion.

Cooperation modes: pure commission, pit fee + commission, guarantee + commission, dedicated session, mixed session, short video + live, clip authorization, group leader distribution, creator matrix testing.

Always calculate creator net profit and maximum commission before commitment.

## Qianchuan structure

Use plan types by job:

- Live-room conversion.
- Short video to live room.
- Short-video product sale.
- Product-card promotion.
- Search harvest.
- Creator-content boosting.
- Old-customer retargeting.
- High-intent audience.
- New-product testing.
- Hero-SKU scaling.

Testing period: small budget, multiple creatives, multiple selling points, multiple plans.

Scaling period: concentrate on high CTR, high CVR, high ROI assets and split marginal audiences.

Stable period: control marginal cost and increase natural traffic share.

Decay period: replace creative, audience, or SKU before blindly increasing bid.

## Xingtu and shelf/search

Use Xingtu for awareness, product seeding, scenario mindshare, search stimulation, store/product-detail visits, add-to-cart, transaction, SPU audience assets, and secondary boosting value.

Use shelf/search/product card to catch active demand after short video, live, creator seeding, mall browsing, and repeat visits. Optimize title keywords, cover click rate, price benefit, reviews, sales base, detail conversion, SKU structure, activity labels, store score, fulfillment, and after-sale.
