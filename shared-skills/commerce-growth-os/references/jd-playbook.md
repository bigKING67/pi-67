# JD Playbook

## Load when

Use this reference for JD/Jingdong channel strategy, JD flagship operation, search/shelf conversion, 3C/home appliance/premium consumer trust landing, logistics promise, or when the user asks whether to add JD as a channel.

## JD role

JD is strongest when the category needs trust, delivery certainty, authentic channel perception, after-sale confidence, or parameter comparison. Treat JD as a shelf/search/trust channel first, not a pure content seeding channel.

Common channel jobs:

- Capture high-intent search demand after Douyin/Xiaohongshu seeding.
- Provide official/authentic purchase certainty.
- Convert parameter-heavy or service-heavy categories.
- Support gift, stock-up, and fast-delivery occasions.
- Build review, Q&A, service, and logistics trust assets.

## Fit judgment

JD is usually a good fit when:

- Users search by brand, model, parameter, ingredient, or scenario.
- Delivery speed, authenticity, warranty, invoice, or after-sale matters.
- The product has a clear standard SKU or model.
- The brand can support official-store trust and review accumulation.
- Gross margin can carry JD traffic cost and promotion mechanics.

Be cautious when:

- The brand depends mainly on impulse content conversion.
- The product needs long emotional education before purchase.
- A direct price comparison would damage Tmall/Douyin price lines.
- The SKU has weak reviews, unstable supply, or high after-sale risk.

## SKU jobs

| SKU role | JD job | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Standard hero SKU | Search and trust landing | Direct price comparison |
| Parameter/model SKU | Rational comparison and conversion | Requires clear proof and service |
| Bundle SKU | AOV lift and price separation | Harder comparison if bundle logic is unclear |
| Gift/occasion SKU | Festival, business, family gift | Packaging and delivery expectation |
| Service SKU | Product + warranty/service assurance | Service capacity risk |
| Repurchase SKU | Stock-up, refill, member repeat purchase | Needs reminder and price control |

## Price and channel control

Do not blindly copy Tmall or Douyin live prices to JD. Define:

- JD official price.
- JD daily coupon price.
- JD campaign price.
- JD member/old-customer price.
- JD bundle price.
- Minimum profit price.
- Forbidden floor price.

Use bundle specs, service/warranty value, delivery promise, gifts, and member benefits before visible price cuts.

## Search and shelf diagnosis

Inspect:

- Title/model/parameter keywords.
- Main image click clarity.
- Price and coupon visibility.
- Review count and negative review reasons.
- Q&A and after-sale questions.
- Detail-page proof and parameter table.
- Delivery promise and stock location if relevant.
- Store score, service response, invoice/warranty clarity.
- Search ranking and competitor price/spec comparison.

## Paid media decision

Before adding JD paid media, check:

- Search intent is real.
- Product page and reviews can convert.
- Channel net profit after fees, coupons, gifts, and returns is acceptable.
- Price does not undercut Tmall/Douyin standard lines.
- Inventory and service are ready.

Stop when spend creates clicks without payment, or when price/coupon mechanics breach the minimum profit price.

## Category notes

- 3C/smart hardware: parameters, warranty, official authenticity, comparison reviews, Q&A, and after-sale are central.
- Premium food/gifts: delivery speed, packaging, shelf life, review photos, and gifting scenarios matter.
- Beauty/personal care: JD can serve official trust and stock-up, but Xiaohongshu/Tmall may carry more education/review depth depending on category.
- Home appliance/home care: logistics, installation, warranty, and service promise can dominate conversion.
