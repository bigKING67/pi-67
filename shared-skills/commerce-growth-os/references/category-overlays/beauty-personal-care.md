# Beauty and Personal Care Overlay

## Use when

Use for skincare, hair/scalp care, body care, personal cleansing, fragrance-adjacent personal care, or beauty devices when category-specific evidence and risk matter.

## Demand and trust

- Demand is driven by a visible or felt concern, desired routine, identity, sensory experience, and confidence in sustained use.
- Trust depends on ingredient/formula logic, usage method, suitable audience, expected timeframe, realistic proof, safety, and consistent experience.
- Separate immediate sensory benefit, cosmetic appearance, routine benefit, and longer-term outcome.

## Product and evidence

- Record concern, skin/hair type, contraindications, usage frequency, regimen position, packaging compatibility, and shelf life.
- Evidence may include formula rationale, instrumental or consumer tests, expert explanation, usage demonstration, and verified user experience.
- Claims must match evidence scope, sample, method, duration, and applicable regulation.

## Commercial risks

- Samples, gifts, trial sizes, creator seeding, refunds, leakage, breakage, and repeat cadence materially affect economics.
- Avoid building one hero SKU without a credible routine, replenishment, or adjacent-product path.

## Operations and supply risks

- Monitor formula/packaging stability, batch consistency, leakage, oxidation, pumps, temperature, expiry, and complaint traceability.
- Refund diagnosis should separate allergy/irritation, expectation mismatch, usage error, packaging failure, and logistics damage.

## Compliance boundaries

- Do not imply diagnosis, treatment, permanent change, guaranteed efficacy, or universal suitability without valid authority and evidence.
- Sensitive groups, scalp/skin conditions, active ingredients, devices, and before/after materials require conservative review.

## Lifecycle

- Model trial, activation, routine formation, replenishment, cross-sell, seasonal need, and churn separately.
- Repeat rate must be evaluated against expected depletion and treatment/routine cycle.

## Specialist application

Commercial Strategy owns price and economics; Brand owns meaning and trust; Content owns proof translation; Growth owns acquisition and replenishment experiments; Operations owns service/refund execution; Analytics owns cohorts and complaint evidence.
