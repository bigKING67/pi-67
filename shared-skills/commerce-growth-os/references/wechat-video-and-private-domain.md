# WeChat Video and Private Domain Playbook

## Load when

Use this reference for WeChat Channels/video account, mini program, community/private-domain operation, member lifecycle, repeat purchase, old-customer recall, or when the user asks how to connect public traffic to private retention.

## Role

WeChat/private domain is not a magic free-traffic pool. Its primary job is retention, trust deepening, replenishment, education, service, and lifecycle value. Use it after the business has a clear SKU role and reason for repeat contact.

Common jobs:

- Convert high-intent users who need consultation or trust.
- Retain first-time buyers into replenishment/member cohorts.
- Support launch education and customer-service certainty.
- Recall old customers for refill, routine, seasonal, or gift occasions.
- Reduce paid-media dependence by increasing LTV.

## Fit judgment

Strong fit when:

- The category has repeat purchase, service, education, or stage lifecycle.
- Users need consultation, usage guidance, or post-purchase care.
- The brand can provide ongoing content and customer-service capacity.
- There is a clear member benefit beyond coupons.

Weak fit when:

- Product is one-off with no reason for follow-up.
- The team only wants to move users into a group and push discounts.
- No staff/process exists for tagging, content cadence, service, or privacy compliance.

## Funnel design

Use this sequence:

1. Public touchpoint: Douyin, Xiaohongshu, Tmall, JD, offline, package insert.
2. Trust reason: usage guide, warranty, refill reminder, member benefit, service, education.
3. Capture surface: mini program, official account, group, 1:1 service, member form.
4. Tagging: category interest, SKU bought, scenario, repurchase cycle, complaint risk.
5. Lifecycle action: education, refill reminder, old-customer coupon, bundle upgrade, seasonal/gift push.
6. Review loop: repeat purchase, unsubscribe, complaint, content engagement, service load.

## Content and service jobs

| Job | Content/action | Metric |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Onboarding | Usage guide, suitable/unsuitable cases, service contact | First 7-day engagement, complaint reduction |
| Education | Routine, scenario, comparison criteria, proof explanation | Save/read/comment quality |
| Refill | Replenishment reminder, refill pack, member coupon | Repeat purchase rate, repurchase interval |
| Upgrade | Bundle, stage-up product, premium line | AOV, second-order gross profit |
| Service | Q&A, troubleshooting, after-sale, return prevention | Refund reason, CS response time |
| Advocacy | Review collection, buyer show, referral | Review quality, referral orders |

## Price and privacy rules

- Do not train users to wait for private-domain coupons if it damages public channel price lines.
- Use member-only gifts, refill packs, service value, or bundle upgrades before visible price cuts.
- Keep user tagging and contact frequency compliant with platform and privacy rules.
- Do not import users through deceptive benefits or forced group entry.

## Video account / live role

Use WeChat video/live for:

- Old-customer education and launch explanation.
- Trust-heavy categories needing longer explanation.
- Community/member event conversion.
- Founder/expert/service style content where compliant.

Do not treat WeChat live as a replacement for Douyin live unless the brand already has strong WeChat assets or a service-heavy decision path.

## Review metrics

Track:

- New private-domain members by source.
- Member activation rate.
- Repeat purchase rate and repurchase interval.
- Second-order gross profit.
- Old-customer revenue share.
- Unsubscribe/complaint rate.
- Service workload and response time.
- Review/referral contribution.
