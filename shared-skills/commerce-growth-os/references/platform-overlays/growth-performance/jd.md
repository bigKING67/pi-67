# JD Growth and Performance Overlay

## Use when

Use for JD paid search, product promotion, high-intent audience capture, retargeting, or launch growth experiments.

## Growth jobs

- Capture brand, model, parameter, ingredient, scenario, and category demand.
- Reinforce trust, logistics, service, warranty, and review advantages in the landing experience.

## Required experiment fields

- Search or audience hypothesis, product/model, landing, budget cap, attribution window, payment and contribution metrics.
- Guard against price conflict, low review quality, service overload, warranty issues, and return costs.
- Scale only when search quality, conversion, contribution, inventory, and after-sale remain stable.

## Currentness

Current ad products, bidding modes, audience capabilities, and report fields require authoritative verification.
