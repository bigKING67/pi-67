# Pinduoduo Growth and Performance Overlay

## Use when

Use for Pinduoduo volume tests, value-channel acquisition, channel-exclusive packs, subsidy decisions, or clearance growth.

## Growth jobs

- Acquire value-oriented demand only with explicit channel role and SKU separation.
- Use channel-exclusive size, bundle, packaging, or service to reduce direct price comparison.

## Required experiment fields

- SKU separation, price floor, subsidy source, logistics, refund, service, unauthorized distribution, and channel-conflict guardrails.
- Stop when volume requires violating the forbidden floor price or harms other channels.
- Scale only when channel net profit, price governance, reviews, fulfillment, and repeat behavior remain acceptable.

## Boundary

Commercial Strategy must approve economics and channel conflict before any paid or promotional scale.
