# Xiaohongshu Growth and Performance Overlay

## Use when

Use for paid Feed, Search, creator-content amplification, search interception, audience expansion, or content-to-commerce experiments.

## Growth jobs

- Feed creates discovery and expands qualified audience signals.
- Search captures active intent generated by content, category demand, and competitor comparison.
- Paid amplification should extend proven content, not conceal weak relevance or proof.

## Required experiment fields

- Keyword or audience hypothesis, content asset, landing path, budget cap, search/engagement/landing metrics, and compliance guardrails.
- Stop when interaction fails to create search or qualified landing, or when competitor targeting creates compliance or brand risk.
- Scale only after organic and paid signals produce durable search and conversion value.

## Currentness

Current Juguang or other commercial capabilities, targeting, and competitor-term policies require authoritative verification.
