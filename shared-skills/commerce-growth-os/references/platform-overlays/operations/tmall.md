# Tmall Operations Overlay

## Use when

Use for Tmall flagship operations, shelf/search landing, product pages, campaign execution, reviews, membership, fulfillment, or conversion diagnosis.

## Operating system

- Maintain title, main image, SKU, price, benefits, detail page, reviews, Q&A, buyer show, service, stock, and delivery consistency.
- Separate traffic, hero, profit, bundle, repurchase, channel, and campaign SKU roles.
- Prepare campaign calendar, price approval, gifts, inventory, customer service, and post-campaign recovery.
- Operate member service and repeat purchase without uncontrolled discounts.

## Exceptions

Escalate price inconsistency, weak reviews, unavailable stock, delayed delivery, service-score decline, claim risk, or rising refunds. Current platform workflow requires verification.
