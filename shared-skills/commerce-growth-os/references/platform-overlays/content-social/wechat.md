# WeChat Content and Social Overlay

## Use when

Use for WeChat official-account content, video-account organic content, community communication, member education, or private-domain content cadence.

## Platform job

- Deepen trust and service after public-platform discovery.
- Support education, member value, replenishment, launches, events, and customer relationships.

## Content system

- Separate service, education, founder/expert, product-use, member, event, and replenishment content.
- Match content depth and frequency to lifecycle state and consent.
- Link content to mini-program, store, service, community, or membership actions.

## Guardrails

- Respect privacy, consent, contact frequency, opt-out, and claim boundaries.
- Do not turn every community or message into a promotion.
- Measure useful engagement, service load, retention, complaints, unsubscribe, and repeat behavior.
