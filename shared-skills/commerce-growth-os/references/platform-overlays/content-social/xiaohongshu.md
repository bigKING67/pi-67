# Xiaohongshu Content and Social Overlay

## Use when

Use for Xiaohongshu seeding, search mindshare, KOL/KOC programs, creator briefs, note systems, or off-platform commerce landing.

## Platform job

- Build trust, scenario discovery, comparison, category education, and durable search assets.
- Translate consumer language into brand, category, scenario, problem, comparison, and competitor keyword coverage.

## Content system

- Map audience x scenario x question x evidence before choosing creators.
- Use experience, routine, tutorial, comparison, list, myth-correction, and diary formats.
- Assign authority, vertical, lifestyle, experience, KOC, and long-tail creators distinct jobs.
- Define titles, body proof, comment handling, search terms, landing destination, rights, and refresh cadence.

## Guardrails

- Avoid identical scripts, false experience, unsupported claims, artificial comments, and hard selling in every note.
- Measure search lift, saves, qualified comments, landing signals, content reuse, and audience asset value.
- Current commercial products and targeting capabilities require authoritative verification.
