# Kuaishou Analytics Overlay

## Metric contract

Separate creator, short-video, live, paid, shelf/search, repeat viewer, buyer, refund, service, and relationship signals.

## Diagnostic chain

Content exposure -> qualified live entry -> watch/trust -> product click -> payment -> refund -> repeat session/customer.

Review contribution across repeated sessions, creator dependence, price, fulfillment, complaints, and repeat behavior rather than judging one burst alone.
