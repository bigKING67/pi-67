# Xiaohongshu Analytics Overlay

## Metric contract

Separate organic, creator, paid Feed, paid Search, brand account, search, and landing signals. State when off-platform conversion cannot be deterministically attributed.

## Diagnostic chain

Exposure -> qualified engagement -> save/comment quality -> search lift -> brand/category keyword movement -> landing -> downstream conversion.

Include content reuse, creator quality, audience assets, and search durability. Do not infer incremental sales from interaction alone.
