# Kuaishou Playbook

## Load when

Use this reference for Kuaishou live/short-video strategy, trust commerce, value-driven livestream, lower-tier market expansion, creator matrix, or when the user asks whether to add Kuaishou as a channel.

## Role

Kuaishou is often stronger in trust-based creator relationships, community familiarity, repeat selling, and practical value explanation. Treat it as a relationship/conversion channel, not just another Douyin clone.

## Fit judgment

Kuaishou can fit when:

- Product explanation benefits from creator trust and repeated exposure.
- Price/value pack can be compelling without breaking brand anchors.
- The category has visible use, replenishment, household value, or clear benefit.
- The brand can provide creator-specific packs and stable fulfillment.

Be cautious when:

- Brand is highly premium and depends on strict price perception.
- The only plan is to copy Douyin assets and discount harder.
- Gross margin cannot carry commission, benefit, and refund risk.
- The product has high claim/compliance or after-sale complexity.

## Channel jobs

- Creator live: trust conversion, repeat selling, community proof.
- Brand live: explanation, service, retention, stable conversion once audience exists.
- Short video: practical demo, problem-solution, creator trust buildup.
- Shelf/search: catch active demand after content/live exposure.
- Private/member: replenishment and old-customer retention when repeat purchase exists.

## Assortment and price

Use:

- Creator-exclusive pack.
- Value bundle with clear spec difference.
- Household stock-up pack.
- Trial-to-standard path.
- Clearance pack only if separated from premium hero SKU.

Avoid putting the same hero SKU below Tmall/Douyin/JD price anchors. Use pack/gift/spec/service separation before visible discounting.

## Creator fit

Check:

- Audience match and repeat buyer quality.
- Creator trust style: household, practical, vertical expert, value, or lifestyle.
- Historical category refund/complaint pattern if available.
- Script compliance and claim discipline.
- Ability to explain pack difference and suitable/unsuitable users.

## Profit model

Before cooperation, calculate:

```text
Kuaishou channel net profit =
GMV
- product cost
- platform fee
- creator commission
- pit/service/group cost
- gift/sample cost
- fulfillment cost
- refund loss
- content/authorization cost
```

If net profit is negative, label the cooperation as acquisition, clearance, or trust-asset budget and define payback.

## Metrics

Track:

- Creator live GMV and channel net profit.
- New-customer share and repeat buyer quality.
- Refund and complaint reasons.
- Product click and payment CVR.
- Fan/member contribution.
- Content reuse value.
- Price conflict with other channels.

## Stop and scale rules

Stop or renegotiate when refund, price conflict, or after-sale pressure exceeds plan, or when creator GMV only works below forbidden floor price.

Scale when contribution profit, repeat purchase, fulfillment, and price separation stay stable across multiple sessions.
