# Home Paper and Daily Necessities Category Pack

## Load when

Use this pack for tissue, toilet paper, wet wipes, kitchen paper, trash bags, laundry detergent, cleaning products, dishwashing, bathroom/kitchen consumables, storage consumables, and other household daily necessities. For premium fragrance-led home care, also consider `premium-home-fragrance-and-care.md`.

## Category operating logic

Home paper and daily necessities sell value, certainty, convenience, and repeat purchase:

- Logistics before discount: weight, volume, leakage, breakage, and shipping subsidy can erase gross margin.
- Spec clarity before traffic: count, weight, sheet size, concentration, refill volume, and pack content must be explicit.
- Repeat purchase before one-time GMV: household consumption cadence and stock-up habits are the real LTV.
- Channel-specific pack before low-price expansion: value channels need isolated specs or packs to avoid price conflict.
- Review quality before scale: missing pieces, false spec perception, leakage, scent sensitivity, and damaged packaging hurt conversion.

## SKU matrix

| SKU role | Example | Job | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trial SKU | Small pack, sample, mixed scent | First purchase | Low AOV and high freight share |
| Hero family pack | Tissue box/carton, laundry liquid, cleaning set | Main GMV and stock-up | Heavy logistics and price comparison |
| Value pack | Larger count, simpler packaging, channel-exclusive spec | Match price-sensitive demand | Can reset price memory |
| Refill pack | Refill bag, replacement roll, refill wipes | Repurchase and margin | Leakage/damage risk |
| Combo pack | Kitchen + bathroom, laundry + softener, paper + wipes | AOV and household solution | Complex fulfillment |
| Premium scenario pack | Baby/pet/sensitive-skin/kitchen-specific if compliant | Differentiation | Requires proof and careful claims |
| Clearance pack | Old packaging, short-date if applicable, isolated bundle | Inventory digestion | Complaint and brand-value risk |

## Price and logistics strategy

Calculate by order, not only by gross margin:

- Product cost.
- Platform fee.
- Fulfillment and shipping by weight/region.
- Packaging reinforcement.
- Gift/sample cost.
- Refund/replacement loss.
- Coupon/subsidy.
- Customer-service cost for missing/damaged/leaking orders.

Define:

- Official shelf price.
- Family-pack price.
- Value-channel pack price.
- Campaign stock-up price.
- Member/replenishment price.
- Minimum profit price.
- Forbidden floor price.

Use pack size, refill, scent/flavor, package count, channel-exclusive barcode/SKU, or gift difference to prevent direct comparison across Tmall/JD/Douyin/Pinduoduo/Kuaishou.

## Channel split

- **Tmall**: flagship trust, standard pack, review base, brand search, campaign stock-up, member repurchase.
- **JD**: fast delivery, family stock-up, official/authentic trust, heavy-item service certainty.
- **Douyin short video**: cleaning demo, usage scenario, family stock-up story, before/after only when compliant.
- **Douyin self-live**: bundle explanation, stock-up benefits, pack count clarity, repeat purchase.
- **Talent livestream**: works for value packs only if freight/refund economics and pack specs are locked.
- **Xiaohongshu**: home organization, cleaning routine, sensitive-use caution, aesthetic storage, family/pet/baby scenarios.
- **Pinduoduo**: value pack, clearance, old packaging, or channel-exclusive spec with strict price/profit controls.
- **Kuaishou**: relationship trust and practical value explanation for household bundles.
- **WeChat/private domain**: replenishment reminders, family subscription, old-customer pack, community group-buy only with price control.

## Content and keyword directions

Build content around:

- Scenario: family stock-up, rental home, pet household, baby household, kitchen grease, bathroom scale, laundry, travel pack.
- Proof: count, size, thickness, absorbency, concentration, cleaning process, package contents, before/after where compliant.
- Convenience: one-month household consumption, storage, refill, subscription, fast delivery.
- Trust: real unboxing, weighing/counting when useful, packaging protection, review photos.

Xiaohongshu and search keyword clusters:

- Product + use: tissue, toilet paper, wet wipes, kitchen paper, laundry detergent, cleaning spray, trash bag.
- Scenario: family, pet, baby, kitchen, bathroom, rental, dorm, stock-up, moving.
- Proof: thickness, no residue, scent, absorbency, concentration, count, size, refill.
- Decision: "worth stocking", "comparison", "review", "one month use", "not easy to leak".

## Douyin script directions

Use:

```text
Household problem -> visible demo/spec count -> pack value explanation -> logistics/quality reassurance -> repeat-purchase or stock-up reason -> landing
```

Avoid vague "super cheap" scripts. Convert price into unit economics, household use days, pack contents, and service certainty.

## Tmall/JD/search landing

Landing must show:

- Exact count, weight, size, concentration, scent, and package contents.
- Applicable and unsuitable scenarios where relevant.
- Shipping/packaging protection and replacement rules.
- Review photos showing received pack, size, scent/texture, cleaning result, and damage/leakage status.
- Q&A for count, sheet size, scent strength, baby/pet use, allergy/sensitivity, shipping damage, and replenishment cycle.

## Talent livestream rules

Before booking:

1. Calculate channel net profit with commission, pit fee, freight, packaging, gift, refund/replacement, and subsidy.
2. Lock pack specification and prevent "same SKU lower price" comparison.
3. Set a maximum order volume by warehouse and CS capacity.
4. Prepare scripts for count, spec, shipping, leakage, missing-piece, and replacement questions.

Do not use talent live or value channels to sell the same hero family pack at a visibly lower all-in price unless the objective is explicit clearance and the price-memory risk is accepted.

## Compliance and refund risk checklist

Flag:

- False or confusing count, weight, size, concentration, or "family month supply" claims.
- Unsupported sterilization, antibacterial, deodorizing, baby-safe, pet-safe, skin-safe, or allergy-related claims.
- Before/after cleaning demos that imply guaranteed outcomes without proof.
- Leakage, broken pump, damaged carton, missing pieces, short shipment, scent complaints, skin sensitivity, and poor CS response.
- Heavy shipping regions that turn profitable orders into losses.

## Repurchase and LTV

Design:

- Trial -> family pack.
- Family pack -> monthly/quarterly replenishment.
- Cleaning SKU -> refill pack.
- Seasonal campaign -> stock-up bundle.
- Private domain/member -> replenishment reminders and household checklist.

Track repurchase by household type, consumption cadence, pack size, region freight, refund/replacement reason, and channel cohort.

## Default output additions

For home paper/daily-necessities full plans, always add:

1. Pack specification and unit-price table.
2. Logistics/freight/refund sensitivity.
3. Channel-exclusive pack and price-control plan.
4. Stock-up and replenishment path.
5. Review/Q&A plan for count, leakage, damage, scent, and replacement.
6. Pinduoduo/value-channel conflict guardrail if low-price channels are considered.
