# Premium Pet Category Pack

## Load when

Use this pack for premium pet food, pet snacks, pet care, pet supplies, cats, dogs, pet-owner lifestyle, palatability, subscription, and premium pet health/care categories.

## Category operating logic

Premium pet categories rely on owner trust and repeat use:

- Pet health trust before conversion: ingredient transparency, suitability, palatability, and real-use proof matter.
- Trial before subscription: owners often need trial packs or small sizes before committing.
- Lifestyle identity before price war: premium pet buyers pay for care, confidence, aesthetics, and long-term fit.
- Repurchase before one-off GMV: main profit comes from routine consumption, replenishment, and subscription.
- Conservative claims before aggressive function: avoid treatment or disease claims unless approved and verified.

## SKU matrix

| SKU role | Premium pet example | Job | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trial SKU | Trial pack, small bag, sample set | Reduce first-use and palatability risk | Low AOV if not connected to subscription |
| Hero SKU | Core food, snack, care product | Main trust and GMV anchor | High review and proof requirement |
| Function SKU | Coat, digestion, dental, joint, urinary-support style | Match problem scenario | Health-claim and suitability risk |
| Subscription SKU | Monthly pack, replenishment bundle | LTV and retention | Needs stable supply and clear use cycle |
| Lifestyle SKU | Premium packaging, aesthetic accessories, gift pack | Brand image and gifting | May underperform without scenario content |
| Multi-pet pack | Larger size or family pack | Raise AOV | Shipping weight and storage issues |
| Talent-exclusive pack | Hero SKU + trial/gift | Creator conversion and price separation | Gift cost and fairness risk |

## Price and margin strategy

Protect premium trust:

- Tmall: official trust, reviews, ingredient/proof modules, Q&A, subscription and member benefits.
- Xiaohongshu: real owner scenario, pet response, selection logic, routine education.
- Douyin short video: visual use, feeding/care demo, before-after only where compliant.
- Douyin self-live: explanation, trial pack, subscription bundle, limited gift.
- Talent livestream: owner-fit creator, exclusive pack, no direct floor-price comparison.

Use trial packs, subscription benefits, gifts, and bundles before visible discounting.

## Creator and content fit

Creator types:

- Cat/dog vertical creators with real pet ownership.
- Multi-pet family creators for routine and consumption proof.
- Pet nutrition or pet-care explainers using conservative language.
- Lifestyle creators for premium packaging, gifting, and home aesthetics.
- KOC owner review matrix for palatability and repeat-use trust.

Avoid creators who overpromise disease treatment, attack competitors, or create unsafe feeding/care demonstrations.

## Keyword directions

Build keywords by:

- Species and life stage: cat, dog, kitten, puppy, senior pet, small/large breed.
- Scenario: picky eating, travel, grooming, dental care, seasonal shedding, home odor.
- Ingredient/proof: only if substantiated.
- Benefit words: use cautious lifestyle or support language, not treatment promises.
- Owner identity: new pet owner, multi-pet household, premium pet care.
- Brand/product words: defend after creator and content seeding.

Map keywords across Xiaohongshu notes, Tmall title/detail/Q&A, Douyin product card, and search landing.

## Tmall and search landing

Tmall must handle risk questions:

- Ingredient, origin, test/proof, suitability, feeding/use method, and unsuitable cases.
- Reviews with pet type, age, size, usage cycle, and palatability.
- Q&A for transition period, dosage, storage, shipping damage, returns, and subscription.
- Member path for replenishment reminders and subscription packs.

## Talent livestream rules

Talent livestream works when:

- Creator's pet type and audience match the SKU.
- Product demonstration is safe and easy to understand.
- Trial pack or exclusive bundle reduces first-purchase risk.
- Reviews and proof assets are ready.
- Inventory and fulfillment can support repeat purchase, not only one burst.

Calculate net profit and commission ceiling before booking. If net profit is negative, define whether the cooperation buys trial users, search assets, or creator proof.

## Compliance risk checklist

Verify current legal/platform rules before final claims. Flag:

- Disease treatment or drug-like implication.
- Guaranteed health result.
- Unsafe feeding/care demonstration.
- Unsupported ingredient, origin, or certification claims.
- Before-after claims without proof.
- Absolute safety or "all pets" suitability.

Safer pattern:

```text
Describe species/stage fit, routine use, observable experience, transition method, unsuitable cases, and available proof conservatively.
```

## Repurchase and LTV

Design retention around pet routine:

- Trial -> standard size.
- Standard size -> subscription or monthly pack.
- Function SKU -> routine bundle.
- Multi-pet household -> larger pack.
- Member -> replenishment reminders, pet profile, and old-customer benefits.

Track repurchase by pet profile and SKU role, not only channel GMV.

## Default output additions

For premium pet full plans, always add:

1. Pet suitability and claim boundary.
2. Trial-to-subscription path.
3. Palatability/review proof plan.
4. Tmall Q&A and review landing.
5. Creator safety and fit checklist.
6. Shipping, storage, refund, and complaint watchlist.
