# Premium Food and Beverage Category Pack

## Load when

Use this pack for premium food, beverage, nutrition snacks, coffee, tea, functional snacks, gift boxes, origin/craft stories, taste proof, and premium pantry or lifestyle consumption.

## Category operating logic

Premium food and beverage sells taste, trust, scenario, and gifting:

- Taste proof before scale: visual appeal, real tasting, texture, aroma, and repeat purchase matter.
- Origin/craft before price: ingredient, production method, place, and brand story support premium value.
- Scenario before generic SKU: office, family, fitness, afternoon tea, festival gift, party, and self-reward scenarios require different packs.
- Logistics before campaign burst: shelf life, breakage, leakage, temperature, and gift packaging can destroy reviews.
- Compliance before health language: avoid treatment, weight-loss, and exaggerated functional claims.

## SKU matrix

| SKU role | Premium food/beverage example | Job | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trial SKU | Tasting pack, mini box, sample cup | Lower first purchase barrier | Low AOV without bundle upgrade |
| Hero SKU | Core flavor or best-selling pack | Main GMV and proof point | Easy competitor comparison |
| Assortment SKU | Multi-flavor set, tasting collection | Raise AOV and discovery | Inventory and preference complexity |
| Gift SKU | Festival gift box, premium packaging | Margin and occasion capture | Packaging damage and seasonality |
| Stock-up SKU | Office/family pack, monthly box | Repurchase and AOV | Logistics weight and storage risk |
| Functional SKU | Light nutrition, low-sugar, protein, wellness snack | Differentiation | Health-claim and proof risk |
| Member SKU | Subscription, limited flavor, refill | LTV and community | Needs cadence and novelty |

## Price and margin strategy

Use pack architecture to protect premium value:

- Tmall: flagship trust, search capture, review base, gift packaging, detail proof.
- Xiaohongshu: lifestyle scenario, taste notes, gifting recommendation, ingredient/craft story.
- Douyin: sensory demo, tasting reaction, limited pack, live bundle.
- Talent livestream: creator-exclusive tasting/gift set instead of direct floor price.
- Member/private: subscription, limited flavor, replenishment, festival reminders.

Prefer smaller trial packs, gift value, multi-flavor sets, and member benefits before visible discounting.

## Creator and content fit

Creator types:

- Food reviewers who can explain taste and texture.
- Lifestyle creators for coffee, tea, office, home, party, or gifting scenes.
- Fitness/light-eating creators only when claims are substantiated.
- Gift recommendation creators for festival and relationship scenarios.
- KOC tasting matrix for flavor preference and repeat-purchase proof.

Avoid creators who rely on unsupported health claims, exaggerated origin claims, or unrealistic taste comparisons.

## Keyword directions

Build keywords by:

- Category: coffee, tea, snack, chocolate, cereal, beverage, gift box.
- Taste/texture: aroma, crisp, smooth, rich, light, low-sweetness, fresh.
- Scenario: office, afternoon tea, breakfast, travel, party, festival, gifting.
- Origin/craft: only if proof exists.
- Audience: premium gift buyer, office worker, fitness lifestyle, family pantry.
- Function: low sugar, protein, caffeine, nutrition style wording only where substantiated.

Map keywords into Xiaohongshu titles, Tmall search/detail, Douyin product cards, and campaign landing pages.

## Tmall and search landing

Tmall must convert seeded interest:

- Title covers brand, category, flavor, scenario, pack size, and gift occasion.
- Main image communicates taste, pack value, and packaging.
- Detail page separates taste profile, origin/craft proof, ingredient list, storage, shelf life, delivery, gifting, and reviews.
- Reviews should show real pack, portion, taste, freshness, and gift presentation.
- Member path should cover subscription, replenishment, limited flavors, and festival reminders.

## Talent livestream rules

Talent livestream works when:

- Tasting or sensory proof can be shown quickly.
- Pack value is clear without damaging flagship price.
- Shelf life, inventory, packaging, and shipping are locked.
- Creator audience matches taste or gifting scenario.

Calculate net profit before booking and include packaging, sample, breakage, refund, and logistics costs.

## Compliance and fulfillment risk checklist

Verify current legal/platform rules before final claims. Flag:

- Weight-loss, treatment, or health-result implication.
- Unsupported origin, certification, or ingredient superiority.
- False scarcity or exaggerated craft claims.
- Shelf-life, storage, cold-chain, breakage, leakage, and delayed-shipment risk.
- Gift packaging mismatch or missing gift components.

Safer pattern:

```text
Describe taste, texture, scenario, ingredient/proof basis, storage, shelf life, and suitable users without medical or guaranteed-result claims.
```

## Repurchase and LTV

Design retention around usage and occasion:

- Trial -> hero pack.
- Hero pack -> multi-flavor set.
- Gift buyer -> self-use or repeat gift reminder.
- Stock-up pack -> subscription or monthly box.
- Member -> limited flavor, festival pre-order, and replenishment reminders.

Track repeat purchase by scenario and flavor, not only total channel GMV.
