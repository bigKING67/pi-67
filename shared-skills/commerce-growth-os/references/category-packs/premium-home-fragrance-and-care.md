# Premium Home Fragrance and Care Category Pack

## Load when

Use this pack for premium home fragrance, scent, home care, premium cleaning, laundry fragrance, aesthetic home, giftable home products, and lifestyle home care.

## Category operating logic

Premium home fragrance and care sells atmosphere, texture, and trust:

- Scent/experience before function-only claims: premium buyers care about atmosphere, emotion, and home identity.
- Aesthetic content before commodity price comparison: packaging, home scene, giftability, and routine matter.
- Trial before full-size: scent and texture preferences need low-risk entry.
- Refill before one-off GMV: long-term profit comes from refills, seasonal scents, and member routines.
- Safety boundaries before aggressive claims: avoid unsupported disinfection, health, pregnancy, pet, or baby-safe claims.

## SKU matrix

| SKU role | Premium home example | Job | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trial SKU | Scent sample, mini spray, travel size | Reduce scent-choice risk | Fragmented inventory |
| Hero SKU | Core fragrance, detergent, care product | Main GMV and brand scent memory | Easy scent preference mismatch |
| Scent set | Multi-scent discovery set | Raise AOV and exploration | Harder explanation |
| Refill SKU | Refill pack, replacement cartridge | Repurchase and margin | Price comparison |
| Gift SKU | Premium gift box, seasonal scent set | Image and gifting | Packaging damage |
| Routine bundle | Laundry + fragrance + home care set | Lock routine and raise AOV | Too complex if benefits unclear |
| Seasonal SKU | Holiday scent or limited edition | Campaign and novelty | Forecasting risk |

## Price and margin strategy

Protect premium perception:

- Xiaohongshu: home aesthetics, scent story, lifestyle scene, gift guide, routine content.
- Tmall: flagship trust, scent descriptions, reviews, Q&A, refill/member path.
- Douyin: fast scene transformation, before/after home atmosphere, live bundle.
- Talent livestream: gift set or scent discovery set, not a bare floor-price SKU.
- Member/private: refill, seasonal scent, replenishment, old-customer benefits.

Prefer samples, discovery sets, refill benefits, and gift value before visible discounting.

## Creator and content fit

Creator types:

- Home lifestyle and interior creators.
- Fragrance/scent reviewers using sensory but careful language.
- Cleaning and laundry routine creators for demo products.
- Gift recommendation creators.
- KOC home routine matrix for scent preference and real use.

Avoid creators who overstate disinfection, health, baby/pregnancy/pet safety, or permanent effects.

## Keyword directions

Build keywords by:

- Scent: woody, floral, citrus, clean, tea, musk style descriptors where applicable.
- Scene: bedroom, bathroom, laundry, entryway, office, gift, hotel-like home.
- Emotion/style: relaxing, premium, clean home, ritual, aesthetic.
- Function: deodorizing, cleaning, softening, freshening only when supported.
- Audience: renters, new home, pet household, gift buyer, office worker.
- Brand/product words: protect after content seeding.

Map keywords into Xiaohongshu notes, Tmall title/detail, Douyin product-card titles, and customer-service scripts.

## Tmall and search landing

Tmall should answer scent and safety questions:

- Title and main image show scent family, usage scene, pack size, and product role.
- Detail page explains scent notes, use method, duration, refill path, suitable/unsuitable scenarios, and safety boundaries.
- Reviews should include scent preference, room size/use amount, persistence, packaging, and gift feedback.
- Q&A should cover allergies/sensitivity carefully without medical promises.

## Talent livestream rules

Talent livestream works when:

- The creator can describe scent and scene credibly.
- The pack is giftable or discovery-oriented.
- Product demo is visual enough for live conversion.
- Claims stay within supported home-care boundaries.

Calculate net profit and include packaging, gift, breakage/leakage, and refund loss. If the session is mainly brand image, label it as image/seeding budget.

## Compliance risk checklist

Verify current legal/platform rules before final claims. Flag:

- Disinfection, antibacterial, health, allergy, sleep, pregnancy, baby, or pet-safe claims without proof.
- Permanent odor-removal or absolute effectiveness.
- Unsupported ingredient or origin superiority.
- Unsafe use scenes.
- Exaggerated before-after or competitor comparison.

Safer pattern:

```text
Describe scent experience, home scenario, correct usage, suitable and unsuitable cases, and available proof conservatively.
```

## Repurchase and LTV

Design retention around routine and season:

- Trial scent -> full-size hero.
- Hero -> refill.
- Refill -> subscription or seasonal scent.
- Gift buyer -> self-use conversion.
- Member -> replenishment, seasonal launch, and home routine bundles.

Track repurchase by scent family and usage scene, not only total GMV.
