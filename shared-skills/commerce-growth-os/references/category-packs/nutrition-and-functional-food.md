# Nutrition and Functional Food Category Pack

## Load when

Use this pack for nutrition food, functional snacks, supplements-adjacent foods, protein products, low-sugar/low-fat products, wellness beverages, meal replacements, and health-positioned consumer food. For medical/regulated supplements or drugs, require current legal/platform verification before claims.

## Category operating logic

Nutrition and functional food must balance proof, taste, compliance, and repeat purchase:

- Compliance before benefit aggression: avoid disease treatment, guaranteed health outcomes, or exaggerated body-result claims.
- Taste and routine before scale: users may try for function but repurchase for taste, convenience, and habit.
- Proof before trust: ingredient list, nutrition facts, test reports, certifications, and suitable/unsuitable users matter.
- Trial before subscription: reduce first-purchase risk, then move to monthly/stock-up packs.
- Refund/review before paid scale: taste mismatch, digestive discomfort, packaging damage, and expectation gaps can hurt reviews.

## SKU matrix

| SKU role | Nutrition/functional example | Job | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trial SKU | 3-day pack, mixed flavor trial | Lower taste/routine risk | Low AOV without upgrade |
| Hero SKU | Core protein/snack/beverage | Main proof and GMV | Easy health-claim overreach |
| Flavor set | Multi-flavor pack | Taste discovery and AOV | Inventory complexity |
| Routine pack | 14/30-day pack | Habit and LTV | Higher first-purchase barrier |
| Scenario SKU | office, fitness, breakfast, night snack | Match use case | Needs clear scenario proof |
| Gift/stock-up SKU | family/office/festival pack | AOV and campaign | Logistics and shelf-life pressure |
| Member SKU | subscription, limited flavor, refill | Repurchase | Requires cadence and novelty |

## Price and margin strategy

Use pack architecture:

- Tmall/JD: official trust, ingredient/nutrition proof, reviews, search capture.
- Xiaohongshu: lifestyle routine, taste, scenario education, safe claim boundaries.
- Douyin: sensory demo, convenience, routine explanation, live bundle.
- Talent livestream: trial/flavor/routine pack with clear claim boundaries.
- Private/member: subscription, flavor rotation, replenishment reminders.

Prefer trial packs, flavor sets, subscription benefits, and member gifts before cutting visible shelf price.

## Creator and content fit

Creator types:

- Food/taste reviewers for palatability.
- Fitness/lifestyle creators using compliant language.
- Office/breakfast/parenting scenario creators.
- Ingredient/nutrition explainers only with substantiated proof.
- KOC tasting and routine-use matrix.

Avoid creators who promise weight loss, disease improvement, medical outcomes, guaranteed body change, or fear-based health claims.

## Keyword directions

Build keywords by:

- Category: protein snack, low-sugar snack, nutrition bar, cereal, beverage, meal replacement style wording.
- Scenario: office, fitness, breakfast, late-night, travel, family pantry.
- Taste/texture: chocolate, coffee, crunchy, smooth, low sweetness, satiety wording where compliant.
- Ingredient/proof: protein, fiber, sugar, calorie, certification only if substantiated.
- Audience: office worker, fitness lifestyle, student, parent, silver lifestyle only when compliant.
- Brand/product words for defense after seeding.

## Douyin script directions

Use:

```text
Scenario -> taste/convenience demo -> nutrition/proof basis -> safe benefit language -> suitable/unsuitable users -> pack reason -> landing
```

Show real eating/drinking, packaging, portion, storage, and routine. Avoid before-after body claims unless legally verified and properly substantiated.

## Tmall/JD/search landing

Landing must show:

- Nutrition facts and ingredient list.
- Taste/flavor explanation.
- Suitable and unsuitable users.
- Storage, shelf life, serving size.
- Test/certification proof where available.
- Reviews showing taste, portion, routine, freshness, and package condition.
- Q&A for allergies, sugar, calories, caffeine, protein, digestive discomfort, and delivery.

## Talent livestream rules

Talent/live works when:

- Taste demo is convincing.
- Pack value is clear.
- Claims stay within food/nutrition boundaries.
- Inventory, shelf life, package integrity, and customer-service scripts are ready.

Calculate net profit including sample cost, breakage/leakage, refund loss, commission, pit fee, and content authorization.

## Compliance red zones

Verify current legal/platform rules before final claims. Flag:

- Disease treatment, prevention, or cure implication.
- Guaranteed weight loss, fat loss, muscle gain, sleep, blood sugar, immunity, or medical outcome.
- Misleading low-sugar/zero-sugar/low-fat claims.
- Unsupported ingredient superiority.
- Before-after body comparison.
- Expert endorsement without valid basis.

Safer pattern:

```text
Describe nutrition facts, taste, routine scenario, serving suggestion, suitable/unsuitable users, and available proof without medical or guaranteed-result promises.
```

## Repurchase and LTV

Design:

- Trial -> hero pack.
- Hero pack -> routine 14/30-day pack.
- Flavor set -> preferred flavor subscription.
- Office/family pack -> monthly replenishment.
- Member -> flavor rotation, recipe/routine content, replenishment reminders.

Track repurchase by flavor, scenario, and cohort, not only channel GMV.

## Default output additions

For nutrition/functional food full plans, always add:

1. Compliance-safe benefit language.
2. Taste and routine proof plan.
3. Trial-to-subscription path.
4. Tmall/JD proof and Q&A landing.
5. Shelf-life, packaging, allergy, and refund watchlist.
6. Sensitivity on refund loss and repeat purchase payback.
