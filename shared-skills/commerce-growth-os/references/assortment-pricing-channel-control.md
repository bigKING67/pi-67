# Assortment, Pricing, and Channel Control

## Load when

Use this reference for SKU design, price ladder, channel conflict, creator-exclusive products, campaign products, or "which product should sell where" questions.

## SKU roles

| Role | Job | Common channel |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Traffic SKU | Low barrier, new-customer entry | Live, short video, first order |
| Hero SKU | Main GMV and proof point | Douyin, Tmall, talent livestream, paid search |
| Profit SKU | Margin recovery | Self-live, Tmall, member/private |
| Bundle SKU | Raise AOV and hide direct price comparison | Live, talent livestream, campaign |
| Repurchase SKU | Raise LTV | Tmall member, private domain, subscription/periodic purchase |
| Image SKU | Premium brand mindshare | Xiaohongshu, Tmall flagship, brand campaign |
| Talent-exclusive SKU | Give creators a story and economics | Douyin talent livestream, group leader distribution |
| Channel-exclusive SKU | Reduce cross-channel price conflict | Douyin, Tmall, Xiaohongshu, distributors |
| Campaign SKU | Explode during 618/Double 11/New Year | Campaign period |
| Gift SKU | Increase benefit perception without cutting shelf price | Live, talent, member |
| Clearance SKU | Inventory liquidation | Value creators, controlled campaign |
| Test SKU | Validate new concept | Small-budget content/search/live test |

## Price ladder

Define these lines before channel execution:

- Official list price.
- Daily transaction price.
- Coupon price.
- Self-live price.
- Talent-live exclusive price.
- Tmall daily price.
- Tmall campaign price.
- Xiaohongshu landing price.
- Member repurchase price.
- Bundle price.
- Channel-exclusive price.
- Minimum profit price.
- Forbidden floor price.

Use three red lines:

```text
Brand mindshare price: what users believe the brand is worth.
Daily transaction price: sustainable normal selling price.
Minimum profit price: below this, the sale is not economically valid.
```

## Prefer dark subsidy over visible price cuts

Use gifts, more volume at same price, bundles, second item discount, coupons, member coupons, creator-exclusive gifts, live-room benefits, campaign stock-up packs, and old-customer coupons before cutting the visible standard price.

## Channel job design

- Xiaohongshu: value, trust, scenario, search decision; do not lead with lowest price.
- Douyin short video: pain point, demo, immediate purchase reason.
- Douyin self-live: time-limited benefit, bundle, stable conversion.
- Douyin talent livestream: creator-exclusive pack and gift; avoid direct comparison with daily SKU.
- Douyin shelf/search: standard hero SKU, high-click product card, active demand.
- Tmall: standard SKU, brand trust, reviews, search, member repurchase.
- Private/member: replenishment, refill, periodic purchase, old-customer benefit.
