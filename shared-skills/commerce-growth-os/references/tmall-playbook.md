# Tmall and Wanxiangtai Playbook

## Load when

Use this reference for Tmall/Taobao shelf operations, product pages, search keywords, Wanxiangtai, member repurchase, campaign planning, or Tmall conversion diagnosis.

## Tmall role

Tmall should provide search capture, product conversion, brand trust, review assets, flagship-store certainty, and member repurchase. It is often the trust and repurchase anchor for users seeded on Xiaohongshu or Douyin.

## Product diagnosis

Inspect:

- Title keywords.
- Main image CTR.
- Price competitiveness without price-line damage.
- Detail-page persuasion.
- Review count and quality.
- Q&A, buyer show, negative concerns.
- SKU structure.
- Coupon and activity mechanism.
- Sales base.
- Store score.
- Customer-service response.
- Fulfillment speed.
- Member benefits.
- Search ranking.
- Favorite/add-to-cart rate.
- Payment CVR.

If Xiaohongshu/Douyin seeding rises but Tmall does not convert, inspect brand-word/category-word landing, review base, price consistency, and detail-page proof.

## SKU jobs

- Standard SKU: search landing and price reference.
- Hero SKU: main GMV.
- Bundle SKU: AOV lift.
- Profit SKU: margin recovery.
- Member SKU: repurchase.
- Campaign SKU: event burst.
- Image SKU: brand mindshare.

## Keyword matrix

- Brand words: defense.
- Category words: new-customer acquisition.
- Benefit/function words: conversion.
- Scenario words: seeding landing.
- Audience words: precision.
- Competitor words: cautious interception.
- Long-tail words: low-cost conversion.
- Campaign words: event harvest.

Map keywords into title, search ads, content landing, detail-page modules, and campaign pages separately.

## Wanxiangtai decision rules

Use Wanxiangtai by job:

- Product full-site promotion: scale proven hero SKU.
- Keyword promotion: capture active search demand.
- Audience promotion: acquisition, recall, repurchase.
- Favorite/add-to-cart retargeting: improve payment conversion.
- Old-customer repurchase: improve LTV.
- Campaign preheat: seed favorites, carts, members.
- Campaign burst: harvest brand/category/campaign words.

Rules:

- New product: test keywords and audiences before full-site scale.
- Hero SKU: combine full-site promotion with keyword capture.
- High-repurchase product: prioritize audience promotion and old-customer recall.
- High-search product: prioritize keyword promotion.
- After Xiaohongshu seeding: strengthen brand and category word landing.
- Before campaigns: accumulate favorites, carts, and member assets.
- During campaigns: harvest brand, hero, activity, and category demand.
