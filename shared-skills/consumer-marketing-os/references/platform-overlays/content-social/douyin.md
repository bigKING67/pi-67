# Douyin Content and Social Overlay

## Use when

Use for Douyin organic short video, creator content, Xingtu content assets, search stimulation, or content-to-live/product-card handoff.

## Platform job

- Create interest through fast scenario recognition, demonstration, proof, and personality.
- Produce reusable creator and brand assets for organic distribution and paid amplification.
- Stimulate search and route qualified users to live, product card, store, or owned channels.

## Content system

- Separate problem, scenario, demonstration, authority, comparison, lifestyle, objection, and handoff content families.
- Define hook, proof, product role, CTA, landing destination, claim boundary, and asset rights.
- Use creator tiers by audience and content job, not follower size alone.
- Keep brand constants stable while testing hooks, pacing, proof, people, and formats.

## Guardrails

- Do not optimize only for views or engagement.
- Monitor qualified clicks, search lift, conversion quality, refund signals, fatigue, and reuse value.
- Current Xingtu or platform capabilities require authoritative verification.
