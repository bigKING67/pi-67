# Tmall Commerce Content Overlay

## Use when

Use for Tmall product-page content, flagship-store brand expression, buyer proof, search-message consistency, or campaign landing content.

## Platform job

- Convert active demand with clear product hierarchy, proof, price consistency, reviews, Q&A, and service confidence.
- Preserve brand trust for users arriving from social content or search.

## Content system

- Align title, main image, detail modules, comparison, proof, FAQ, buyer show, and campaign page with the same message hierarchy.
- Separate traffic information, decision proof, objection handling, and after-sale expectations.
- Reuse social proof only when rights and context remain valid.

## Guardrails

- Do not hide delivery, usage, compatibility, size, claim, or refund-relevant information.
- Measure product-detail engagement, add-to-cart, payment conversion, review quality, Q&A themes, and refund reasons.
