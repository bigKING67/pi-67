# JD Commerce Content Overlay

## Use when

Use for JD product proof, parameter comparison, service communication, high-intent search landing, or trust-focused product content.

## Platform job

- Convert users who value authenticity, specification clarity, delivery certainty, warranty, and after-sale confidence.

## Content system

- Make model, parameter, ingredient, compatibility, service, warranty, delivery, and Q&A information explicit.
- Use comparison and scenario proof that reduces high-intent purchase uncertainty.
- Align social seeding claims with the JD product page and service promise.

## Guardrails

- Do not copy Tmall content without accounting for JD search, service, logistics, and parameter expectations.
- Measure search click, product-detail conversion, Q&A, review quality, service contacts, returns, and warranty issues.
