# Tmall Growth and Performance Overlay

## Use when

Use for Wanxiangtai, keyword promotion, audience retargeting, campaign media, product growth, or Tmall conversion experiments.

## Growth jobs

- Capture active brand, category, scenario, and product demand.
- Re-engage visitors, cart users, buyers, and member segments with controlled frequency.
- Use paid media only after product page, price, reviews, service, and inventory can convert traffic.

## Required experiment fields

- Keyword/audience, product, landing, offer, budget cap, attribution window, payment metric, profit and refund guardrails.
- Diagnose search quality, click, product detail, add-to-cart, payment, refund, and repeat behavior separately.
- Scale based on marginal contribution rather than platform-reported GMV alone.

## Currentness

Current product names, optimization modes, audience assets, and report fields require authoritative verification.
