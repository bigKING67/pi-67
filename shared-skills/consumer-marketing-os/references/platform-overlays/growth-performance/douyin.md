# Douyin Growth and Performance Overlay

## Use when

Use for Qianchuan, paid short-video or live traffic, product-card promotion, search capture, creator-asset amplification, retargeting, or marginal ROI diagnosis.

## Growth jobs

- Test audience, message, creative, product, price, and landing combinations with bounded budgets.
- Scale proven combinations while monitoring marginal economics and natural-traffic contribution.
- Use search, product card, store, or live as distinct destinations with distinct conversion contracts.

## Required experiment fields

- Objective, audience, asset, destination, budget cap, attribution window, primary metric, refund and profit guardrails.
- Stop when marginal performance, conversion quality, refunds, inventory, or service fail the agreed thresholds.
- Scale gradually only when contribution profit and operational readiness remain stable.
