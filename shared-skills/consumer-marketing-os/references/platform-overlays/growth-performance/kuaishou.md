# Kuaishou Growth and Performance Overlay

## Use when

Use for Kuaishou paid traffic, creator amplification, live acquisition, value-bundle growth, or relationship-commerce scaling.

## Growth jobs

- Amplify creator trust and repeated product explanation rather than copying another platform's media structure.
- Connect short video, creator, live, shelf/search, and repeat purchase.

## Required experiment fields

- Creator/audience hypothesis, content asset, live or shelf destination, budget, commission, price, refund, and fulfillment guardrails.
- Stop when scale depends on price conflict, excessive commission, or after-sale pressure.
- Scale only after repeated sessions show stable contribution, trust, delivery, and repeat behavior.

## Currentness

Current commercial products, targeting, and reporting capabilities require authoritative verification.
