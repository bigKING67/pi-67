# WeChat Growth and Lifecycle Overlay

## Use when

Use for video-account paid growth, mini-program conversion, private-domain acquisition, membership, replenishment, repeat purchase, or recall.

## Growth jobs

- Convert trusted audiences through video, mini-program, service, community, and membership touchpoints.
- Move customers through consented lifecycle states rather than broadcasting the same promotion to everyone.

## Required experiment fields

- Lifecycle segment, message, channel, frequency, destination, offer, holdout, LTV, unsubscribe, complaint, and privacy guardrails.
- Stop when fatigue, unsubscribe, complaints, service load, or margin deteriorate.
- Scale only when incremental retention or repeat value is demonstrated.

## Currentness

Current advertising, data, mini-program, and video-account capabilities require authoritative verification.
