# Campaign Brief Contract

## Required fields

- Campaign objective and business context.
- Priority audience and consumer tension.
- Single-minded proposition and reasons to believe.
- Desired belief, feeling, and behavior change.
- Creative idea and message hierarchy.
- Brand constants and flexible variables.
- Owned, earned, paid, shared, offline, creator, and commerce roles.
- Hero asset, derivatives, platform adaptations, and source-file requirements.
- Timeline, budget, owner, approvals, rights, expiry, and dependencies.
- Primary metric, guardrail metrics, test plan, and post-campaign learning.

The brief must make tradeoffs. A list of every possible message is not a strategy.
