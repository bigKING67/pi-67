# Growth Experiment Contract

## Required fields

- Decision and hypothesis.
- Baseline, expected mechanism, and material assumptions.
- Audience, eligibility, channel, and destination.
- Creative, offer, placement, or landing variable.
- Budget cap, duration, sample limits, and seasonality.
- Attribution window, holdout or comparison design, and known limitations.
- Primary metric and guardrails for profit, refunds, brand, inventory, and service.
- Stop, scale, modify, and inconclusive rules.
- Owner, result, confidence, learning, and next experiment.
