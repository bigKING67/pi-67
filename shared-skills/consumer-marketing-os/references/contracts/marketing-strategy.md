# Marketing Strategy Contract

Use this contract for integrated or annual marketing plans.

## Required fields

- Business objective and decision horizon.
- Marketing objective and brand objective.
- Confirmed facts, assumptions, and evidence date.
- Priority audience, buyer, influencer, and excluded audience.
- Consumer insight, category frame, positioning, promise, and reasons to believe.
- Desired journey change: awareness, consideration, search, conversion, retention, or advocacy.
- Brand communications, content/social, paid growth, CRM, commerce, and offline channel jobs.
- Annual theme or campaign architecture.
- Test, scale, brand-building, retention, and reserve budget buckets.
- Measurement framework, attribution limits, guardrails, and review cadence.
- Owners, dependencies, approval gates, risks, and next decision.

Do not fill missing facts with fabricated market data. Use explicit assumptions and list the evidence needed to replace them.
