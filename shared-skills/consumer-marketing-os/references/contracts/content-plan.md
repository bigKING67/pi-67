# Content Plan Contract

## Required fields

- Platform and platform job.
- Audience, journey stage, insight, and content objective.
- Content pillar, topic, format, message, proof, and CTA.
- Creator role and selection criteria.
- Search terms and commerce or owned-channel destination.
- Cadence, volume, production owner, approval, and refresh rule.
- Usage, edit, derivative, paid-amplification, and expiry rights.
- Brand, claim, compliance, and price-consistency checks.
- Primary metric, quality guardrails, learning question, and next decision.
